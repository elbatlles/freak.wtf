Grafix Gestió Informàtica is a small IT consultancy where I worked from 2012 to 2020 — my first real professional experience, and where I learned to work with many different types of clients and businesses.

The work was varied: clients ranged from clothing shops to butchers, food businesses, and local retailers. For each one I'd build or maintain their online store, create budgets, and handle whatever they needed on demand. No two projects were the same.

The main stack was WordPress, PrestaShop, PHP, and CodeIgniter. I built and customised e-commerce stores, developed small internal management apps tailored to each client's specific needs, and handled ongoing maintenance and iterations.

Beyond pure development, I also got involved in digital marketing strategy for some clients — SEO basics, product catalogue setup, promotional campaigns. Working with non-technical clients on tight budgets taught me how to scope, communicate and deliver pragmatically.

It was a broad generalist role that gave me solid fundamentals across the full web stack and an early understanding of what clients actually need versus what they ask for.
