At Kumux, I worked on frontend development and data visualization — translating complex visual data into interfaces that stayed clear and fast.

The main challenge was performance: slow chart renders made the UI feel broken even when the data was correct. I worked on optimizing rendering pipelines and keeping the visual logic decoupled from data fetching.
