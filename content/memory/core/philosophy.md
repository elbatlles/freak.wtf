I optimize for clear tradeoffs instead of perfect abstractions. If a decision is hard to explain, it is probably not good enough yet.

I prefer small, composable changes over large rewrites. Most long-term wins come from consistency, not heroics.

When a product surface feels artificial, users notice immediately. Simplicity is part of trust.
