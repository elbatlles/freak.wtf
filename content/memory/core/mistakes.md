I have over-engineered early versions by solving hypothetical future problems. That usually delayed feedback and reduced learning speed.

I have also shipped things too quickly without enough observability. Fast shipping without visibility creates silent risk.

The durable lesson is balance: ship small, keep instrumentation close, and communicate uncertainty early.
