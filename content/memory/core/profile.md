I am a software engineer focused on building products that are useful, maintainable, and honest about their constraints.

I care about velocity, but not at the cost of clarity. I prefer systems that are easy to reason about under pressure.

Across projects, I keep returning to the same idea: software quality is not a luxury, it is a multiplier for people and teams.
